# Barbershop & Beauty Salon Platform

> Part of the [worlds-biggest-software-project](https://github.com/worlds-biggest-software-project) initiative.
>
> An AI-native, open-source platform for salon and barbershop operations: online booking, stylist management, POS, and loyalty.

The Barbershop & Beauty Salon Platform is a vertical SaaS for independent barbershops, salons, and multi-location chains. It unifies appointment scheduling, walk-in queue management, integrated payments, client records, and marketing in a single open codebase, addressing pain points that today are scattered across ten or more proprietary commercial products.

---

## Why Barbershop & Beauty Salon Platform?

- Every leading platform analysed (Fresha, Mangomint, Squire, Boulevard, Vagaro, GlossGenius, Barberly, Zenoti, Timely, Mindbody) is closed-source proprietary SaaS with no open core.
- Open API access is restricted or paywalled on most platforms — Vagaro charges extra for webhooks, Boulevard gates its API to enterprise tier, Zenoti restricts API access to authorised developers, and Fresha, GlossGenius, Barberly, and Timely have no documented public API.
- Pricing extremes leave gaps: enterprise platforms like Zenoti require $5,000–$20,000 implementations, while affordable tiers (GlossGenius, Timely) intentionally exclude features needed by multi-staff operations.
- Cross-platform parity is weak — Squire's desktop web interface is repeatedly cited as significantly less functional than its mobile app, and similar gaps exist across competitors.
- Underserved capabilities include AI-powered client style memory, dynamic demand-aware pricing, proactive rebooking prediction, multi-jurisdiction licence tracking, and OSHA-linked chemical inventory compliance — none of which incumbents execute well.

## Key Features

### Scheduling & Booking

- Appointment calendar with multi-staff lanes, colour coding, and drag-and-drop rescheduling
- Online self-booking via embeddable widget, shareable link, and social booking integrations
- Walk-in queue management with live wait-time estimation and in-shop TV queue board
- Self-check-in kiosk on iOS or Android tablet with service and staff selection
- Multi-service, multi-provider booking optimisation to minimise gaps and back-and-forth slot hunting

### Payments & POS

- Integrated POS supporting chip, swipe, contactless, Apple Pay, and Google Pay
- No-show and late-cancellation protection through deposit and prepayment collection
- Tip management built into the checkout flow
- Product inventory tracking with supplier ordering and consumption tracking

### Client & Staff Management

- Client profiles with visit history, service notes, formulas, and photo documentation
- Staff calendar, timesheets, clock-in/out, commission tracking, and permissions
- Loyalty programme, referral campaigns, and membership/package management
- Staff licence expiry tracking and compliance alerts across multi-jurisdiction operations

### Marketing & Communication

- Automated SMS and email appointment reminders and confirmations
- Email and SMS marketing campaigns with smart audience segments (birthday, lapsed client)
- Two-way unified messaging across SMS, email, and WhatsApp in one inbox
- Review management and social media post templates

### Multi-Location & Reporting

- Multi-location management with unified reporting
- Real-time analytics and financial reporting dashboard
- Role-based access for franchise owners, location managers, front desk, and stylists

## AI-Native Advantage

AI capabilities target the specific gaps incumbents do not address well: a persistent client style and preference profile that surfaces colour formulas, cut notes, and product preferences automatically at check-in to any stylist; smart rebooking prediction that proactively messages clients before their typical rebook window closes; dynamic demand-aware pricing that fills slower slots while protecting peak-period revenue; and an AI receptionist that answers calls, books appointments, and responds to FAQs outside business hours. AI-assisted chemical inventory tracking links product usage to OSHA SDS records, flagging expiry, disposal requirements, and reorder points.

---

## Tech Stack & Deployment

The platform targets cloud SaaS deployment with a self-hostable option, exposing a public REST API and webhook ecosystem from day one — a deliberate contrast to incumbents that restrict or paywall developer access. Integration surfaces include embeddable booking widgets, social booking links (Instagram, Facebook, Google Business Profile), payment processing, and accounting connectors. HIPAA-compliant client charting is on the roadmap to enable medspa adjacency.

## Market Context

Fresha leads global mindshare with over 450,000 beauty professionals across 120 countries on a zero-subscription, payment-commission model. Subscription incumbents range from $11/user/mo (Timely) and $24/mo (GlossGenius) at the low end, through $30–$90/mo (Vagaro) and $165–$375/mo (Mangomint), up to custom enterprise quotes plus $5,000–$20,000 implementation fees (Zenoti). Primary buyers are independent barbershops and salons, multi-location chains, and franchise operators; deregulation across multiple US states is gradually expanding the addressable market for solo-tier software.
